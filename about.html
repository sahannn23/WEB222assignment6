Hello! I’m a web development student who is taking WEB222 course in Seneca College with a deep passion for travel, culture, and exploring the world’s hidden gems. For this project, I chose to focus on Sri Lanka—not only because it’s my mother country, but also because it’s a land of incredible diversity, rich heritage, and breathtaking natural beauty. From ancient cities and sacred temples to lush rainforests and pristine beaches, Sri Lanka offers something for every traveler.

This project is a reflection of both my cultural roots and my love for tourism. It highlights some of the must-visit destinations across the island, aiming to inspire others to explore the vibrant history, warm hospitality, and stunning landscapes that make Sri Lanka truly unique.
